SYSTEMS


Data & Records Management

Innovation has been a vital part of our school as we constantly seek ways to improve our work processes and achieve quality results. This has paved the way for advancements in technology, where we strive to enhance and refine the things we do.

Out of the several systems we are working on, three (3) are currently in use.

1. Asset Register


A simple and effective system that keeps record of all assets we have in the school. The system has its proceses and proceedures in updating records, ensuring assets records are in order aged assets are disposed properly. But more importantly, budgets on asstes are controlled and properly managed so we dont purchase duplicate items.

The system is built with advanced MS Excel features to produce the best record keeping, reporting and analytical report.

2. Students Assessment System


The Student Assessment System has been a cornerstone of our school since 2017. We have continually invested in enhancing our academic assessment processes, which have led to significant improvements benefiting the system's users. Manual errors have been minimized, teachers now experience a more streamlined process by simply entering raw marks, parents receive their children's reports on time, and the report cards feature in-depth academic analysis.

In 2022, we redesigned the system to align with SBC standards while simultaneously enhancing the user experience to make the system more user-friendly and effective. The Student Assessment System is continuously undergoing improvements to provide the best possible user experience and deliver high-quality academic analytical reports.
